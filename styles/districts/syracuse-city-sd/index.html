### Understanding Your Child's Needs in Syracuse City SD At Syracuse City SD, we recognize that every child learns differently, and this is especially true for our diverse student population, where 89% are identified as high needs. We prioritize comprehensive bilingual evaluations tailored to each student's linguistic and cultural background, ensuring accurate assessment and placement. Our Committee on Preschool Special Education (CPSE) provides early intervention services that build a strong foundation for future learning and success. Our focus is empowering families with the knowledge and resources they need to actively participate in their child‚Äôs educational journey. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities that come with supporting refugee and immigrant families, and we are committed to providing culturally responsive services. ### Specialized Support for Success We believe that positive behavior support is essential for creating a safe and inclusive learning environment. Our schools implement evidence-based strategies to promote positive behaviors and address challenging ones, focusing on prevention and individualized interventions. Our special education team, comprised of dedicated professionals, collaborate closely with families, teachers, and community partners to develop individualized education programs (IEPs) that are truly tailored to your child's strengths and needs. We are dedicated to fostering a welcoming and supportive atmosphere where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.
