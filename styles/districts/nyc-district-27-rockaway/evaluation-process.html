Requesting a Special Education Evaluation in District 27

Local Context: Serving Our Diverse Communities

District 27 encompasses a diverse range of neighborhoods, including Broad Channel, Howard Beach, Ozone Park, Rockaway, and Woodhaven. Our commitment is to provide appropriate special education services to all students, understanding the unique challenges faced by families in our geographically varied communities.

Of particular concern are transportation logistics for students with Individualized Education Programs (IEPs), especially those residing in more isolated areas like the Rockaways and Broad Channel. Access to related services, such as speech therapy, occupational therapy, and counseling, can also be challenging depending on location. We strive to overcome these barriers to ensure equitable access for all.

The Written Referral Process for Special Education Evaluation

To initiate a special education evaluation for your child in District 27, a formal written referral is required. This letter should be addressed to the CSE (Committee on Special Education) Chairperson at your child's school. The referral should clearly state your concerns about your child's academic, social-emotional, or physical development, and the reasons why you believe a special education evaluation is necessary.

Include specific examples of difficulties your child is experiencing, any interventions or supports that have already been tried, and documentation such as report cards, work samples, or communication from teachers. Be as thorough as possible to ensure the CSE has a comprehensive understanding of your child's needs.

Important: The 60-Day Timeline

Please be aware that once the CSE receives your written referral, they have 60 school days to complete the evaluation process. This includes conducting assessments, holding meetings, and determining your child's eligibility for special education services. It's crucial to submit your referral as soon as you have concerns, as navigating the process can take time. Document everything and keep copies of all correspondence. Contact the CSE Chairperson directly with any questions throughout the evaluation period.
