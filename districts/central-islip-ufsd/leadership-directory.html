Communication Protocol

The "Written Notice" Rule

Never rely on a phone call alone. In New York Special Education law, timelines (like the 60-day evaluation window) only officially start when the district receives a request in writing.

  • Always follow up a phone call with an email summary.
  • Use the subject line: "Letter of Referral - [Student Name]"
  • Keep a "Communication Log" of dates and names.

When to Escalate?

If you contact the leadership listed above and do not receive a response, follow this escalation ladder:

  1. Day 1: Initial Email/Phone Call.
  2. Day 3: Follow-up Email (CC the Principal).
  3. Day 5: Escalation Email to the Superintendent or District Superintendent.
  4. Day 10: File a "State Complaint" with NYSED if services are denied.
