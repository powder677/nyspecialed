CSE vs. ARD

If you are moving from Texas or other states, terminology changes. The "ARD Committee" is now the Committee on Special Education (CSE). The "FIE" is now the "Psychoeducational Evaluation." We translate the bureaucracy for you.

The 60-Day Timeline

In New York, the district strictly has 60 calendar days from the moment you sign consent to complete evaluations and hold the eligibility meeting. This is your most powerful compliance tool.
